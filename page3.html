History of Peru


Perus coast was home to the Norte Chico civilization, the oldest civilization in the Americas and one of the six cradles of civilization in the world. Its history spans 15 millennia. Plus, after the conquest of the Incas, the Spanish Empire established a Viceroyalty with juridiction over most of its South American domains. Luckily Peru declared independence from Spain in 1821 but only after the Battle of Ayacucho (3 years later)
